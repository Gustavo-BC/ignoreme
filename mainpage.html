About

History

    Good Games Flute Ensemble was founded in 2015 by a group of flutists who shared a passion for video game music while studying various music degrees at the New World School of the Arts. With the combine knowledge and the spirit of the saying "ggs" during matches in games, the group was made. After the formation of the group, the ensemble started performing arrangements of different video game music compositions. Eventually, more flutists were curious about the group and the ensemble had an idea of having a meeting where all flutists can come together and talk, practice, and perform video game music. Now with over 30 active members, the ensemble has become a source of music in video game content.


Objective

    To provide a space in the music community to explore the wonderful compositions of video game music and have flutists gather and perform, practice, and talk among other flutists about anything related to the games that we grew up with or left an impression on us recently.
